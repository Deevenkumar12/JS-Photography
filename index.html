Meet the Founder

Dharma Rao Malladi

Founder · JS Photography

With Over 18+ Years Of Experience Capturing Life's Most Beautiful Moments, M. Dharma Rao, Founder Of JS Photography, Built His Journey With A Single Vision: TO CREATE CINEMATIC, TIMELESS MEMORIES THAT PEOPLE CHERISH FOREVER.

Born In Thamaipaalem Near Kottipalli (AP), Dharma Rao Started His Journey From Humble Beginnings. He Moved To Kovvur (Srinivasapuram, Vambay Colony), where he established his first small studio. With Continuous Learning And Passion, He Embraced New Technology And Introduced Pre-Wedding Songs, Cinematic Wedding Films, and Advanced Video Mixing Techniques.

His Creative Approach Made Him Well-Known For Capturing HIGH-QUALITY WEDDING STORIES. With Zero Negative Feedback, HE EXPANDED TO JS PHOTOGRAPHY STUDIO IN MANGALAVARAPU PETA, RAJAHMUNDRY, along with a branch in Kovvur.

Today, JS Photography Stands As A Modern, AI-Enhanced Studio. Specializing In Weddings, Pre-Wedding Song Shoots, All Type Of Events, And Cinematic Video & Photo Production. Over 500 Weddings And 1000+ Happy Clients Across AP & Telangana.
